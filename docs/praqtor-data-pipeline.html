// 01 — Core Value Proposition

ONE PHOTO IN. DATASET OUT.

The customer provides one real photo of their environment. We return a labeled synthetic training dataset — their specific warehouse, hospital, or job site — rendered across scenarios they cannot safely stage in real life. Forklift near-misses. Worker blind spots. Robot-human intersections. Each image ships with COCO-format bounding box annotations ready for direct ingestion into model training pipelines. Delivered in 72 hours at a fraction of the cost of real-world data collection.

Jensen Huang calls it "one real pixel, AI generates the rest." DLSS renders one real pixel per cluster and reconstructs the frame with neural networks. Our pipeline does the same for training data — one real photograph anchors the scene geometry, lighting, and spatial identity. Everything else — the scenarios, the hazards, the edge cases — is generated synthetically, preserving the customer's actual environment in every frame.
